How to become a Mentor?

To become a trainer at the bootcamp one has to have all the requirements given below to register.

Requirements:

  1. A Mentor's Codeforces rating should be above 1200.

  2. A senior student can be a mentor of a intermediate student if the he/she has a Codeforces rating of a Specialist or above 1400

  3. A senior student can be a mentor of a beginner student if the he/she has a Codeforces rating of a Pupil or above 1200

  4. A senior student can be a mentor of a newcomer student if the she/she has a Codeforces rating of a Newbie or upto 1199

  5. A junior student can be a mentor of a newcomer student if the he/she has a Codeforces rating of a Pupil or above 1200
