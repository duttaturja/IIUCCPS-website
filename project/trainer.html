How to become a Trainer?

To become a trainer at the bootcamp one has to have all the requirements and must follow all the process given below to register.

Requirement:

  1. A trainer should be above 4th semester.

Process:

  1. Create a video tutorial of maximum 10 minutes on any topic of your choice. May be any algorithm topic like Kadane's Algorithm, Dijkstra's Algorithm etc or on basic topic like Time Complexity, Prefix Sum, Two pointers etc.

  2. In addition to this, a doc file should be created containing links to some related problems and some resources.

  3. Upload the video and doc files to your drive and you will be given a file lock in the google form. Change the privacy of these files to "Anyone with the link can view".
